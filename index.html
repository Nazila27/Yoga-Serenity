about us

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by yoga enthusiasts Emily and Michael Anderson, Serenity Yoga Studio was born out of a shared passion for the ancient practice of yoga and its profound impact on modern life.

What started as a small group of friends practicing yoga in a cozy living room has blossomed into a vibrant community and state-of-the-art studio, welcoming people from all walks of life.

Our Mission

At Serenity Yoga Studio, our mission is to create a peaceful sanctuary where individuals can embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery, healing, and growth.

We are dedicated to fostering a holistic approach to well-being, integrating physical, mental, and spiritual practices to help you achieve a balanced and fulfilling life.
